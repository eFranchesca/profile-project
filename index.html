Sea Turtle Rescue

The Turtle Hospital is located at mile marker 48.5 on the Overseas Highway in Marathon, Florida Keys and opened its doors in 1986. The Turtle Hospital contains up-to-date equipment needed to perform a variety of surgeries on different species of sea turtles. More than half of their equipment has been donated by local hospitals, doctors, and environmentally-friendly organizations and individuals.
